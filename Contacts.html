Q: How can I cancel my order?
A: You may inform us directly or contact the cafeteria staff directly. However, this must be carried out the day before the order is supposedly due.

Q: What happens if I am unable to complete the form?

Q: What would happen if the information I filled out in the survey is wrong?
A: In this case, the tech team would take a look to confirm some of the informations. If it is determined that the informations (EX: ID number, name) is incorrect, the order will be automatically rejected.
